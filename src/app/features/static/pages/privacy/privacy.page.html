Privacy Policy

Drafts is a student project built by INSAT RT4. This page explains, in plain language, how the app handles data.

What we collect

  • Account info: email, username, and authentication tokens.
  • Content you create: articles, comments, votes, and uploaded images.
  • Technical data: basic logs needed to run the service (e.g., error logs).

How we use it

  • To let you sign in and keep your session secure.
  • To display your posts and comments and enable voting.
  • To send in-app notifications (for replies, comments, votes).
  • To maintain and debug the application.

What we don’t do

  • We don’t sell your data.
  • We don’t run ads or build advertising profiles.
  • We don’t intentionally collect sensitive personal information.

Storage & retention

As a student project, data retention may be limited. Database resets may occur during development and demos.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at rt4@mail.com.
