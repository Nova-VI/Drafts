About Drafts

Drafts is a place to write, share, and discuss ideas — built with care by students from INSAT (Institut National des Sciences Appliquées et de Technologie), class RT4 (Réseaux et Télécommunications).

What it is

Drafts combines a clean writing experience with a discussion space. Articles can be voted on, and conversations can branch into replies — so good ideas rise and threads stay readable.

  • Publish posts and discuss through comments
  • Vote to highlight the best contributions
  • Real-time notifications to stay in the loop

Where we are

We study at INSAT and built Drafts as part of our learning journey.
